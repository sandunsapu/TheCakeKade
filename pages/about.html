About us

"Creating sweet memories, one cake at a time."

"Satisfy your sweet cravings with our delicious cakes."

Welcome to our cake shop! We are a small, family-owned bakery located in the heart of the city, dedicated to making delicious and beautiful cakes for all occasions. Our story began over 20 years ago, when our founder, Jane, decided to follow her passion for baking and open her own cake shop. Starting from humble beginnings in her home kitchen, Jane's cakes quickly gained a reputation for their incredible flavor and artistry. As our business grew, we moved into our current location and expanded our team to include skilled bakers and decorators. Today, we are proud to offer a wide range of cakes, from classic vanilla to exotic flavors like matcha and lavender. At our cake shop, we believe that every celebration deserves a special cake. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or any other occasion, our team will work with you to create a custom cake that perfectly suits your needs and preferences.We use only the finest ingredients in our cakes, and our decorators are skilled in a variety of techniques, from traditional buttercream frosting to intricate fondant designs. We are passionate about creating cakes that not only look stunning but taste amazing too. Thank you for considering our cake shop for your next celebration. We look forward to working with you to create a truly unforgettable cake!
